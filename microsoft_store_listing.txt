APP NAME

KDP Novels & Storybook Creator

SHORT DESCRIPTION (up to 100 characters)
-----------------------------------------
Write full novels and storybooks with AI — outline, characters, worlds, and chapters in minutes.


FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
Turn your story ideas into complete books — powered by Groq AI.

KDP Novels & Storybook Creator is the ultimate writing companion for authors, storytellers, and aspiring novelists. Describe your concept, and the app handles the rest: it generates compelling titles, subtitles, book premises, full chapter-by-chapter outlines, deep character profiles, world-building lore, front and back matter, and entire chapter drafts — all using state-of-the-art Groq LLM (LLaMA 3.3 70B).

Whether you write fantasy epics, sci-fi thrillers, romance novels, children's books, or business non-fiction, KDP Novels & Storybook Creator adapts to your genre, tone, point of view, and audience — giving you a professional manuscript foundation in the time it used to take just to plan a chapter.

📝 Everything you need, start to finish:
Generate titles and subtitles, write your book premise with one click, build a structured outline, develop characters with backstories and motivations, create rich world-building notes, and watch full chapter drafts appear — one by one, automatically, while you take notes.

✍️ A real manuscript editor, built in:
Every chapter lands in a full prose editor. Search and replace across your entire manuscript, manually refine any section, and regenerate any chapter you're not happy with. Your edits are saved automatically.

📤 Export to Word, PDF, or plain text:
When your book is ready, export the complete manuscript — chapters, front matter, back matter, table of contents, page numbers — as a DOCX, PDF, or TXT file, ready for publishing or self-printing.

🔒 Private and self-hosted:
All your books live on your own machine. Nothing is sent to third-party servers beyond the AI generation calls you explicitly trigger. Your stories stay yours.

🛡️ Content moderation built in:
Readers and reviewers can flag AI-generated content directly from any chapter, and the admin panel lets you review, action, or dismiss reports — keeping your platform responsible and accountable.

📱 Works on desktop, tablet, and as a standalone app:
Fully installable as a Progressive Web App (PWA) — pin it to your taskbar, use it offline for reading and editing, and get a native app-like experience on any Windows device.
